Bodypump

Bodypump is the ideal workout for anyone looking to get lean, toned and fit fast. Using light to moderate weights with lots of repetition. BODYPUMP gives you a total body workout. It will burn up to 540 calories.
Kettlebell Training

Treadmills are mostly used for cardio training. It can be used for more boosting your cardiovascular health, ideal for strengthening your muscles, including glutes, thighs, and calves. You can always optimize the sessions to suit your goals.
Treadmill

Kettlebell engage your entire body at once, unlike dumbbells or barbells they involve large number of repetitions and build strength and endurance. Kettlebells are more suited if you are going to be executing power moves.
Bikes & Cycles

Cycling is a fantastic technique to raise your heart rate without placing undue strain on your joints. Cycling is an aerobic activity. The recumbent bike is the safest way to lose weight and burn those extra calories.
